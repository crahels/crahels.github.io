How to Play Turn It Up?

Scoring

If the player successfully press the button on time, he/she will get the perfect grade. If the player press the button too early or too slowly, he/she will get good grade. If the player push the wrong button or push the correct button lately, he/she will get miss grade and this means player's score will be decreased.

Combo

If the player successfully press buttons continuously, he/she will get additional combo. The combo is used to boost up score because the highest the combo is, the bigger score the player will get as he/she press the buttons accurately. The value of the combo will reset to zero if the button miss.

Life Bar

When the game is on, there will be life bar that indicates the player's energy. Player who successfully press the buttons continuously, will have more energy. The energy has maximum value. If the player cannot press the button accurately, he/she will lose energy.

Game Over

The game will be over if:

  • The player successfully finish the song without running out of energy.
  • The player does not press the buttons accurately and the life bar turn to zero.
  • The player stops the game by pressing "Quit" button.
